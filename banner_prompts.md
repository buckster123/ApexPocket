## Technical Notes

- **Aspect ratio**: 3:1 or 4:1 (wide banner)
- **Recommended size**: 1200x400 or 1600x400 pixels
- **Style**: Should work on both light and dark GitHub themes
- **File format**: PNG with transparency, or JPG
